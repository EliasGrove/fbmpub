Urgent

When we look at history, hindsight likes to tell us we would've acted better, more humanely in the face of great tragedies, more urgently if those suffering asked for our help.

More than 50,000 children have been killed or wounded in Palestine since October 7th, 2023. This means at least one Palestinian child has been killed or wounded every hour, on average, over nearly 23 months of war. By mid-December 2023, 1,000 children had already lost one or both legs, and at least 21,000 children have been left permanently disabled.

These children are asking for your help. We, at Faun by Moonlight, are asking for your help.

We encourage you to donate to the Palestine Children's Relief Fund (PCRF) at https://www.pcrf.net/donate. The PCRF is a nonprofit humanitarian organization dedicated to providing medical care and humanitarian relief to children in the Middle East, regardless of their religion or nationality. Since 1991, they've been sending volunteer medical teams to provide free surgeries and treatments, sponsoring children who need specialized care abroad, building pediatric cancer departments, providing medical equipment and humanitarian aid, and training local doctors and nurses. In Gaza specifically, the PCRF is one of the few organizations still able to provide critical medical supplies, emergency care, and life-saving treatment to injured and sick children.

Readers who send us proof of their donation will be included in our "FBM THANKS" section recognizing you as part of a community that chose to act, as well as a personal email from our editor-in-chief thanking you for your contribution. If you donate $100 or more, we will offer you:

  • One full year of free monthly Volumes
  • Free access to all planned future exclusive content
  • Your name in our "FBM THANKS" section, mentioning your invaluable contribution
  • A personal thank you message from our editor-in-chief

If you are kind enough to donate 200 dollars or more, you will receive access to all benefits above, plus:

  • Free line edits for your work, short story or novel, even if it is not published by us.
  • A letter of recommendation for your future submissions to other magazines

If you donated but don't wish to be named, you can still enjoy all other benefits above.

We understand if you are unable to donate—Times are tough, cost of living is high, and the realities of each of our daily lives can prohibit us from acting the way our hearts wish us to. In these cases, engage and share content discussing these atrocities. Awareness is a powerful tool in its own right.

We hope that you decide to act. We hope we never again need to wait for hindsight to understand what must be done.

Thank you,
Faun by Moonlight Team
