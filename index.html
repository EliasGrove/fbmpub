About Faun by Moonlight Literary Magazine

Faun by Moonlight is an online literary magazine dedicated to exceptional literary fiction. We publish works that resist simple genre classification and aren't scared to bend rules, be unconventional, and above all, creative.

Our Mission

We present readers with literature that both challenges and exceeds expectations. Our specialty is the exceptionally unmarketable - fiction that pushes boundaries and defies easy categorization.

What We Publish

Literary fiction, short stories, novellas, and experimental prose. We seek character-driven narratives with complex themes, unconventional storytelling, and creative innovation. We welcome works that blend genres while maintaining literary quality.

Submission Guidelines

We accept unagented submissions up to 10,000 words. Standard manuscript format required. Submit via email to submissions@faunbymoonlight.com. Response time: 2 weeks. No submission fees.

Payment: $20 for pieces under 5,000 words, $50 for pieces 5,000+ words. We acquire first serial rights with a three-month exclusive period.

Publication Schedule

Monthly digital Volumes featuring 2-3 short stories published through Amazon Kindle. Debut issue launching November 2025. Annual print collections featuring exclusive content, artwork, and essays.

For Writers

No submission fees. Year-round reading period. Quick response times. Payment for accepted work. Helping writers enter the paying market. Building credentials for literary careers.
