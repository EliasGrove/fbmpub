Who We Are

Faun by Moonlight is an online literary magazine dedicated to literary fiction.

We publish works that resist simple genre classification. Works that aren't scared of pushing boundaries, bending rules, but above all, being creative. Our specialty is the exceptionally unmarketable.

Our mission is to present you, our reader, with literature that both challenges and exceeds your expectations.

We are currently reading for our December 2025 issue and putting the finishing touches on our November 2025 inaugural issue. We invite you to return in November to enjoy the phenomenal works we've discovered.

If you're interested in submitting your work, visit our submissions page for details.

About Faun by Moonlight Literary Magazine

Faun by Moonlight is an independent online literary magazine founded in 2025, dedicated to publishing exceptional literary fiction that defies conventional genre boundaries. We are a platform for emerging and established writers whose work resists easy categorization.

Our Editorial Philosophy

We believe in the power of unconventional storytelling. Our specialty is the exceptionally unmarketable - fiction that mainstream publications might overlook because it pushes boundaries, bends rules, or challenges reader expectations. We seek works that prioritize artistic integrity over commercial appeal.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional literary magazines, we embrace the experimental, the genre-defying, and the creatively bold. We publish character-driven narratives with complex themes, unconventional structures, and innovative prose styles. We're not afraid of difficult subject matter or challenging forms.

Our Mission

To present readers with literature that both challenges and exceeds expectations. To support emerging writers who take creative risks. To build a community of readers who appreciate sophisticated, boundary-pushing fiction. To pay writers fairly for their work and help them build credentials in the paying literary market.

Publication History

Launching November 2025 with our debut issue. Monthly digital publications through Amazon Kindle. Annual print collections featuring exclusive content. Year-round submissions accepted.

Editorial Team

Run by volunteers passionate about literary fiction. Committed to quick response times, thoughtful feedback, and supporting writer development. Dedicated to maintaining high editorial standards while embracing creative innovation.

For Writers

We welcome submissions from emerging and established writers. No submission fees. Response within two weeks. Payment for accepted work. We acquire first serial rights with three-month exclusive period. Writers retain all other rights.
