Support Us

Our publication is managed by volunteers committed to publishing great literature. Your support helps us pay writers, purchase the tools we need to produce high quality content, and expand our reach.

Ways to Support Our Work

Read Our Volumes

▼

Our monthly Volumes are available to read in our store faunbymoonlight.store for $4.99 each.

Volunteer

▼

Whether it's editorial work, website development, marketing, beta reading, or other publication tasks, we can always use another helping hand. If you have relevant skills, we welcome your help. Feel free to reach out to us through our contact page.

Purchase our Annual Collections

▼

Every year we compile our best works into a yearly issue. This volume will include exclusive content, artwork, opinion pieces and more.

Buy Us a Coffee

▼

If you'd like to fuel our late-night editing sessions and help cover operational costs, our Buy Me a Coffee page helps keep us caffeinated.

Share Our Content

▼

Recommend stories to other readers, interact with our social media posts, recommend us to writers.

Support Faun by Moonlight Literary Magazine

This page outlines various ways to support Faun by Moonlight, a volunteer-run literary magazine dedicated to publishing high-quality literary fiction. Your contributions help us sustain our mission to pay writers, maintain our platform, and expand our reach within the literary community. Support options include reading our Volumes, volunteering, purchasing annual collections, donating via Buy Me a Coffee, and sharing our content.

Ways to Contribute

Our monthly Volumes, available on our store, feature 2-3 literary fiction stories. Volunteering opportunities include editorial work, website development, marketing, and beta reading—contact us to get involved. Annual collections offer exclusive content, and donations via our Buy Me a Coffee page help cover expenses. Sharing our stories and social media posts also amplifies our reach.

Contact and Social Media

For volunteering inquiries or other support-related questions, visit our contact page or email us. Follow us on X at @faunbymoonlight to stay updated and share our content with the literary community.
