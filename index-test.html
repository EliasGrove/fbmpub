Mission Statement // Est. 2025

Who
We Are

Faun by Moonlight is an online literary magazine dedicated to literary fiction. We are not interested in the polite, the sterile, or the safe.

We publish works that resist simple genre classification. Works that aren't scared of pushing boundaries, bending rules, but above all, being creative. Our specialty is the exceptionally unmarketable.

Our mission is to present you, our reader, with literature that both challenges and exceeds your expectations.

We are currently reading for our December 2025 issue and putting the finishing touches on our November 2025 inaugural issue. We invite you to return in November to enjoy the phenomenal works we've discovered.

If you're interested in submitting your work, visit our submissions page for details.
