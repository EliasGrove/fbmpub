01 The Criteria

What We Want

We are currently open to Literary Fiction and Non-Fiction (Essays, Historical Pieces).

We are looking for works that resist simple genre classification. Works that aren't scared of pushing boundaries. Our specialty is the exceptionally unmarketable.

PLEASE NOTE: We will not publish, nor consider, any works that depict graphic sexual content involving minors. Graphic depictions of extreme violence against minors will be immediately discarded.

Manuscript Requirements

  • Standard format (12pt, Double Spaced)
  • Up to 10,000 words
  • .docx files only
  • Simultaneous submissions accepted
  • No previously published work

AI Policy

We accept submissions that have utilized AI tools for research or editing. However, works primarily generated by AI will not be considered. The creative voice must be authentically yours.
